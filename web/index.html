My name is Fern and I am a fully qualified Reiki Practitioner based in Brighton — I can't wait to share the beautiful gift of Reiki with you.

Reiki is energy, something we are all surrounded by. Throughout our everyday lives we attract or encounter a great deal of negative energy from the foods we eat to the people we meet, which can have a disharmonious effect on us.

Whether regular or occasional, Reiki healing sessions are a great way to look after the bodies we've been blessed with. If nothing else, Reiki is a fantastic way to relax in a world full of stress!

History

The word Reiki is Japanese, rei meaning universal and ki meaning life force. Reiki is a 'laying on of hands' technique that was rediscovered and developed by Dr Mikao Usui at the end of the 19th century during a deep 21 day meditation upon Mt Kurama.

Luckily for us Dr Mikao Usui decided to share this with the world!

What does a Reiki healing session involve?

During a healing session i will channel the universal life force called Reiki through myself and into my client to gently remove any negative energy. I work hands on and off with my clients following a sequence of hand positions.

Before we begin your Reiki session I'll ask you to make yourself comfortable on a massage couch, close your eyes and take some nice deep relaxing breaths. Apart from removing your shoes you will remain fully clothed throughout.

The Reiki energy will flow to wherever it is needed the most — it can usually be felt as a warm or cold sensation, or a tingling feeling, but this can differ from person to person as everybody's experience of Reiki is different.

A Reiki healing session will leave you feeling relaxed and peaceful, it's a beautiful experience!
