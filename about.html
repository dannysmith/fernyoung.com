My Story

My journey began back in 2008. For some time I'd been thinking about studying Swedish Massage. I can't tell you why other than it just kept making an appearance in my life. I eventually decided to act on this and was accepted into a local college only to find myself feeling very disappointed with the course and lecturers. Something just didn't feel right, so despite having waited a whole year to do the course I declined their offer. I was left feeling lost, not knowing why I was here on earth or what path I should take. Months passed by and I spent every day trying to find a career path that I would find satisfying, something that would make others feel good about themselves as well as myself. Then one day at work I happened to mention to a friend of mine that I'd wanted to become a massage therapist. This led the conversation towards a friend of his who is a Reiki Practitioner and something sparked inside of me. I'd heard of Reiki but I knew nothing about it. I've always been a fairly spiritual person so that's when my research into Reiki began. Before long I'd found myself a Reiki Master and was on my way to a better life! A life of love.

Thank you for taking the time to read my story. Fern x
